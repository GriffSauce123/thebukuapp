Core Values

Educational Equity

We are committed to leveling the playing field by providing affordable resources and materials for students from all backgrounds.

Circular Life

Promoting the reuse and repurposing of academic and lifestyle materials to extend their lifecycle and benefit our planet and your pockets.

Community

Fostering connections through peer-to-peer exchanges, bringing people together on campuses nationwide.
